About Me

I'm a class of 2025 student at Sylvania Northview high school and have been taking computer science courses since my sophomore year. Beginning with 2D Game Design and Intro To Computer Science, continuing my junior year with Honors Web Design and Advanced Placement Computer Science Principles, and ending my senior year with Honors System Analysis And Design and Honors Cybersecurity. All of my classes have been taught by Mrs. Tammi Ramsey and I've learned Javascript, Jquery, CSS, Bootstrap, HTML, and am currently learning Shell.

What I'm Working Towards

I (currently) want to carry on with web development and development in general. I'm currently working on the Full-stack development career course on Codecademy where im learning the different skills needed for Front-end and Back-end development.

Why I've Decided

I enjoy playing video games. The thrill, the rush, the calm, the ease; I've always enjoyed being able to go from fighting off hordes of monsters to farming potatoes in a matter of minutes. Going into 6th grade I'd always thought that I wanted to be a game developer, and this thought contiued into my days at high school up to the beginning of my junior year. And then I began my web design class. Web sites look simple enough, and yet they take an incredible amount of time to actually build them how you want. So much so that you can keep learning new techniques and elements of web pages even if you think you've learned them all. Web development just seemed to really interest me, so much so that it's knocked game devolpment right out of my mind, and replaced it.
